Latitude V. Humidity

Humidity appears to have no correlation to the latitude of any given point on earth. A quick hypothesis could be that humidity is linked to elevation and atmospheric pressure rather than latitude on the globe. There is also an oulier in this chart, where a city is showing humidy at or around an index of 300.
